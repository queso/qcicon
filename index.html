Queen City IndieCon is an event in Cincinnati Ohio, featuring literary royalty from the world of Indie authors. There will be two days jam packed with giveaways, food, interesting talks, and a book signing! If that’s not enough for our authors and premium ticket holders you will be invited to several after parties and a ball. That’s right, we will have a ball called the Royal Affair Awards Ceremony. Dress up and enjoy yourself as we watch which authors will be taking home the tiaras.

F.A.Q.

Here you can find answers to some of our more frequently asked questions! Note: If you can’t find the answer to a question you have, please feel free to email us at info@Queencityindiecon.com

Q. Is there a restriction on genre?

A. We will be hosting YA & NA, Fantasy/Paranormal, Contemporary, and Romance authors. We know how much work it takes to be an indie author and we have chosen speakers who can help you learn from their success.

Q. Can I still sign up to be a signing author?

A. Tables are currently sold out ($160), but we are starting a waiting list so email us if you want to be added.

Q: What is the REFUND POLICY for tickets?

You may receive a FULL refund up to 60 days from purchase. After 60 days, you may receive a 50% refund. NO REFUNDS will be given after September 1st , 2014 regardless of when ticket is purchased.

Q: What should I wear to the conference?

A. It’s casual, but Memorial Hall can be a little drafty so wear layers. With this many bodies it could heat up. As for the Royal Affair Ball Awards Ceremony, you are welcome to dress casual, but if you want to kick it up a notch and dress like the royalty you are, feel free to. There will be pictures the night of the awards.

Q: Is dinner included at the Awards?

A. Dinner will not be provided at the Awards. It is highly recommended that you eat something prior to the Awards.

Q: Can dudes attend QCICon?

A. While our event celebrates the strong women of indie publishing we are pleased to open our doors the the studs of indie as well.

These are just some of the questions we have been asked, if we didn’t cover something, please send us an email!
