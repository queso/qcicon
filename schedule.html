Queen City IndieCon is an event in Cincinnati Ohio, featuring literary royalty from the world of Indie authors. There will be two days jam packed with giveaways, food, interesting talks, and a book signing! If that’s not enough for our authors and premium ticket holders you will be invited to several after parties and a ball. That’s right, we will have a ball called the Royal Affair Awards Ceremony. Dress up and enjoy yourself as we watch which authors will be taking home the tiaras.

7:00-8:00am Registration

Pick up your welcome kit and badge at Memorial Hall. You will receive an email shortly before the event with directions. It is a 1/2 mile walk from our hotel and from 7-7:45am we will have volunteer shuttles running back and forth for those of you who may have a hard time walking. We want authors to enjoy the day of talks so table set up will not be until day two.

8:00-8:30am Welcome and Giveaways

In auditorium at Memorial Hall Wendy Owens will address the crowd, give her welcome talk, and we will have sponsor appreciation as well as some giveaways.

8:30-9:15am

Keynote Speaker - Abbi Glines

9:15-9:30am

Break

9:30-10:30am Panel - It's All About the Marketing

From choosing the right categories and tags, to placing ads where it counts, find out how to make your time and dollars count when it comes to marketing.

  • Moderator: Josh Owens
  • Panelists: JA Huss, Ella James, Rachel Marks, MR Polish

10:30-11:30am Panel - That Epic Kind of Love

How to create that couple that can restore a readers faith in true love. Also, let’s talk about the sexy hotties that readers can’t get off their minds. How do we create a leading man that makes a reader melt? What does a reader want in him? What will turn a reader off about a book hunk?

  • Moderator: Sarah Ashley Jones
  • Panelists: Chelsea Fine, Georgia Cates, LP Dover

11:30am - 1pm - LUNCH

Lunch will be provided for all individuals (unless you purchased a no frills/non-assistant ticket). Tables will be set up in the lobby area of Memorial Hall. At 11:30 we will break and filter downstairs to grab a plate. Off from the lobby there will be 2 rooms set up with tables for people to eat at and mingle.

1:00-2:00pm Panel - Show Me the Money

Is free a great marketing ploy or a mistake? How do you know where you fit in the pricing scale from 99 cents to the big bucks? This panel gets down and dirty with pricing and figuring out where your book fits. Find out what has worked for other authors.

  • Moderator: Josh Owens
  • Panelists: Ella James, Robyn Peterman, Chanda Hahn, AnnaLisa Grant

2:00-3:00pm Panel - Is That a Stake in Your Pocket or Are You Just Happy to See Me?

Paranormal is so much more than vampires and werewolves these days. This panel will discuss the vastness of paranormal and fantasy and what makes it something we love as well as something that is here to stay.

  • Moderator: Michele G. Miller
  • Panelists: Denise Grover Swank, Wendy Owens, Tonya Kappes, and Amy Bartol

3:30-4:30pm - I can see the future

NA Contemporary Romance has exploded. So what is in store for 2015? Learn how to be a trend setter rather than chasing the passing craze.
